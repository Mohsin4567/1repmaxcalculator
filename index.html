How to Use the 1RM Calculator

  1. Enter the weight you can lift for multiple reps
  2. Enter the number of reps you performed
  3. Select your preferred weight unit (lbs or kg)
  4. Click "Calculate 1RM" to get your estimates

Why Use Multiple Formulas?

Different formulas work better for different rep ranges and individual variations. By calculating with 5 formulas and averaging them, you get the most accurate estimate possible.

Benefits of Calculating Your 1 Rep Max

  • Track Progress: Monitor your strength gains over time
  • Plan Workouts: Calculate safe training weights based on your max
  • Set Goals: Create realistic strength targets
  • Prevent Injury: Avoid ego lifting with calculated estimates
  • Compare Performance: Benchmark against previous attempts
