📊 About the Formulas

  • Brzycki: Weight × (36 / (37 - Reps)) — Most commonly used
  • Epley: Weight × (1 + Reps / 30) — Popular and reliable
  • Lander: (100 × Weight) / (101.3 - 2.67123 × Reps) — Good for 2-10 reps
  • Lombardi: Weight × Reps^0.10 — Power law based
  • Adams: Weight × (Reps / 30 + 1) / (1.0278 - 0.0278 × Reps) — Alternative
